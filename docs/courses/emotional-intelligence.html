Course Overview

Emotional intelligence is one of the strongest predictors of success in both personal and professional life. This comprehensive 10-week program will help you understand and develop the five key components of EQ.

Through practical exercises, real-world applications, and personalized feedback, you'll develop the emotional skills needed to navigate complex social situations, lead with empathy, and build meaningful relationships.

What You'll Learn

  • The five components of emotional intelligence
  • Self-awareness and emotional self-regulation
  • Reading and responding to others' emotions
  • Empathy and perspective-taking
  • Effective communication in difficult conversations
  • Building trust and rapport
  • Emotional leadership skills
  • Managing team dynamics
