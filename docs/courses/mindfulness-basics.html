Course Overview

This comprehensive 8-week program is designed for complete beginners who want to develop a sustainable mindfulness practice. You'll learn evidence-based techniques drawn from contemplative traditions and modern psychology.

Through a combination of guided practices, interactive exercises, and reflective journaling, you'll build the skills needed to bring mindfulness into every aspect of your life.

What You'll Learn

  • The science behind mindfulness and how it affects the brain
  • Foundational meditation techniques for beginners
  • How to cultivate present-moment awareness
  • Strategies for managing stress and anxiety
  • Mindful breathing and body scan practices
  • Integrating mindfulness into daily activities
  • Building a sustainable personal practice
