Refund Policy

Our policies for cancellations, transfers, and refunds.

Last updated: February 2025

Overview

MyndShift CIC is committed to delivering high-quality training courses. We understand that circumstances may change, and this policy outlines our approach to cancellations, transfers, and refunds.

Cancellation by Participant

More than 14 days before the course

If you cancel your booking more than 14 days before the course start date, you will receive a full refund minus a 10% administration fee.

7-14 days before the course

If you cancel between 7 and 14 days before the course start date, you will receive a 50% refund or the option to transfer to another course date at no additional cost.

Less than 7 days before the course

Cancellations made less than 7 days before the course start date are non-refundable. However, you may transfer your booking to another person or request to attend a future course date (subject to availability and within 6 months of the original booking).

Non-attendance

Failure to attend a course without prior notice will result in forfeiture of the full course fee.

Transfers

You may transfer your booking to another person at any time before the course starts, provided you notify us in writing with the details of the replacement participant.

You may also request to transfer to a different course date, subject to availability. Transfer requests must be made at least 48 hours before the original course date.

Cancellation by MyndShift

In the unlikely event that we need to cancel a course, we will:

  • Provide as much notice as possible
  • Offer you the choice of a full refund or transfer to an alternative date
  • Not be liable for any additional costs you may have incurred (such as travel or accommodation)

Course Changes

We reserve the right to make changes to course content, trainers, venues, or dates if necessary. We will notify you of any significant changes as soon as possible and offer appropriate alternatives.

In-House Training

For in-house training bookings, specific cancellation terms will be agreed upon at the time of booking and outlined in your booking confirmation.
