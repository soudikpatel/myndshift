Supervising First Aid for Mental Health: 2-Days

RQF Level 3 Award

Course Overview

This comprehensive two-day regulated course offers a nationally recognised Level 3 FAA Award and Qualification. Building on the one-day First Aid for Mental Health course, this advanced training provides in-depth knowledge for those who will supervise and support Mental Health First Aiders in the workplace.

The course is available through classroom-based or online delivery and is accompanied by a comprehensive course manual.

Target Group

The regulated course is ideal for Heads of Service, Trainer/Assessors, Teachers, Line Managers, HR professionals, Wellbeing/Mental Health Champions, Health and Safety representatives and anyone who wants to qualify as a Supervising First Aider for Mental Health within the workplace.

Learning Outcomes

During the two-day course, participants will explore a wide range of topics, including:

  • What is First Aid for Mental Health?
  • Identifying mental health conditions
  • Providing advice and starting a conversation
  • Anxiety and Stress
  • Mental health conditions
  • Drugs and alcohol
  • First Aid for Mental Health action plan
  • First Aid for Mental Health in the Workplace
  • Depression
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder
  • Self-harm
  • Suicide
  • Eating disorders
  • Personality disorders
  • Bipolar disorder
  • Psychosis
  • Schizophrenia
