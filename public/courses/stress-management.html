Course Overview

Stress is inevitable, but suffering doesn't have to be. This 6-week course provides you with a comprehensive toolkit for understanding and managing stress in all areas of your life.

Drawing from cognitive-behavioral techniques, mindfulness practices, and positive psychology, you'll learn practical strategies that you can apply immediately to reduce stress and increase your sense of calm and control.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding the stress response and its impact on health
  • Identifying your personal stress triggers
  • Cognitive reframing techniques
  • Relaxation and breathing exercises
  • Time management and prioritization skills
  • Building a support network
  • Creating healthy boundaries
  • Developing long-term resilience
